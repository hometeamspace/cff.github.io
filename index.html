Our Mission

The Mission and Vision of Cancer Fight Foundation is to take utmost care particularly of children born with and / or undergoing treatment of Leukaemia (Blood Cancer) and Adults with Colon Cancer.

A Webinar on MENTAL HEALTH by eminent Psychologist & Psychiatrists spoke several aspects of art therapy, music therapy, yoga, meditation, brain gym, positive body image / discrimination / life after cancer, effect of Counselling in Physical healing of Cancer, etc. the webinar focused on various methodologies to support the Cancer Patients and their families to deal with this ongoing disease

On the occasion of Bengali New Year, CFF will be celebrating Noboborsho in a special way with the cancer patients and survivors in an attempt to gift a colorful and enjoyable day to the patients. The program will include cultural performance and interactive session with renowned psychologists and psychiatrists who will boost the morale of the patients and bring a smile to their faces
